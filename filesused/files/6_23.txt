Three people were arrested for abducting a 13-year-old son of a property dealer in New Delhi, police said on Monday.
"Sandeep, Mukesh and Sunny were arrested from Burari area in north Delhi on Monday evening," a police officer told IANS.
They had abducted Jatin Dhingra from his home in the same area on September 21 and strangled him and dumped his body in a canal in Alipur area, police said.
Police said they demanded Rs 40 lakh from his father.
