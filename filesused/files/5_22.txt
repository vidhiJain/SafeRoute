A witness in an attempt to murder case today escaped unhurt when a man outside the Rohini district courts complex in Delhi opened fire at him.
Court sources said the firing incident occurred near gate 5 of the complex and the assailant, identified as Kishan, was caught at the spot by security personnel.
Rohan, who had come to the court to depose in an attempt to murder case, was the target but he escaped unhurt as the bullet missed him, sources said.
The assailant is being interrogated by the police, sources said.
