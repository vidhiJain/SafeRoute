Two people were arrested for raping a 26-year-old air ticketing woman employee inside a shopping complex in New Delhi, police said on Thursday.
Sunil Sehgal and Sunil Chabra were arrested from their home in Mangolpuri area in north Delhi on Wednesday late night, said an official.
Police said a complaint was lodged on Monday in Mangolpuri police station.
The victim told police that she befriended Sehgal four years ago when he came to the shop, within the shopping complex.
On Monday, Sunil was accompanied by Chabra. The duo took her to an abounded room inside the complex and offered her a drink laced with sedatives, and then raped her, taking turns.
They also threatened her of dire consequences if she told anybody about the assault, the victim said in her written complaint.
