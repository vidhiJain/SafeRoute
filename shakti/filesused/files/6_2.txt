A motorcycle-borne youth allegedly snatched a gold chain from a woman CRPF constable in Dwarka area of southwest Delhi yesterday afternoon.
The incident occurred when 22-year-old Amrita, a constable with 88 women battalion, CRPF and currently posted in Shrinagar was on her way to her friend's residence.
Amrita was in a rickshaw when a motorcycle-borne youth snatched her gold chain and sped away before she could realise anything, police said.
A case has been registered and a manhunt has been launched to nab the culprit, police said.
