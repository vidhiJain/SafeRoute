A 31-year-old man was booked for sexually abusing his five-year-old daughter, police said on Wednesday.
The girl in her statement said her father was sexually abusing her for the past one month.
The girl's mother has registered a case against the accused last evening. He is missing and on the run, a police officer said.
"The girl told her mother about the incident Friday and the mother approached to the police with her family members," said the officer.
The accused also had an illicit affair with his sister-in-law, the girl said in her statement.
