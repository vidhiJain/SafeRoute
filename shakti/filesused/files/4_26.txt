A 22-year-old woman was allegedly raped at gunpoint by a gang of four men in Delhi on Tuesday, the police have said.
The woman, who hails from Mathura in Uttar Pradesh, was returning from Gurgaon on Tuesday when a group of men intercepted the car she was travelling in near National Highway 8 and gang-raped her at gunpoint, the police added.
The men reportedly also took away Rs.5,000 from her.
No arrests have been made in the case yet.
